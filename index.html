  • Hair
  • Nails
  • Sebaceous glands
  • Sweat glands

Major functions: support, protection, movement, storage, and blood cell production

Major Organs:

  • Bones
  • Joints
  • Cartilage
  • Ligaments
  • Tendons

Major functions: movement, posture, and heat production

Major Organs:

  • Skeletal muscles
  • Smooth muscles
  • Cardiac muscles

Nervous System

Major functions: sensory input, integration, and motor output

Major Organs:

  • Brain
  • Spinal cord
  • Nerves

Major functions: hormone production and regulation

Major Organs:

  • Hypothalamus
  • Pituitary gland
  • Thyroid gland
  • Adrenal glands
  • Pancreas

Circulatory System

Major Organs:

  • Heart
  • Blood vessels
  • Blood

Major Organs:

  • Lymphatic vessels
  • Lymph nodes
  • Spleen
  • Thymus
  • Tonsils

  • Pharynx

  • Esophagus
